About Me

Welcome to my portfolio site!

I hold a B.S. in Engineering, Product Design from Stanford University. My career began at Walt Disney Imagineering after I became a finalist in the ImagiNations design competition, for which my partner and I pitched a restaurant concept for a Disney park. My work in themed entertainment took me from Los Angeles to Shanghai, where I oversaw the design, production, and construction of Buzz Lightyear Planet Rescue, an interactive ride in Shanghai Disney Resort.

Prior to beginning my efforts in web development, I also served as Art Director for the Design Studio at Universal Studios Japan, managing design for new installations in the park. I look forward to applying my skills and experience to the tech industry in the near future.
